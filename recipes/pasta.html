Description

White sauce pasta is a creamy and comforting dish made by tossing cooked pasta in a rich b√©chamel sauce, which is made from butter, flour, and milk. The sauce is often seasoned with salt, black pepper, garlic, and sometimes herbs like oregano or basil for added flavor. Grated cheese, typically Parmesan or mozzarella, is often mixed in to enhance the creaminess and taste. Vegetables like mushrooms, bell peppers, or broccoli can be added for texture and nutrition, and it can also be paired with proteins like chicken or shrimp. White sauce pasta is loved for its smooth, cheesy taste and satisfying, indulgent feel.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp margarine
  • 3 tsp all purpose flavour
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients together.
  2. Melt butter and margarine in a saucepan over medium-low heat
  3. Add flour and crumbled bouillon; stir until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until thickened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.
  6. Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  7. Season with white pepper
